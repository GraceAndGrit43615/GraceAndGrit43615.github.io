Mission Statement

Grace and Grit Defense Academy equips civilians by preparing churches and daycare facilities with life-saving knowledge and skills through proven critical response techniques, guided by the values of ALERRT (Advanced Law Enforcement Rapid Response Training).

Rooted in core Christian values of compassion, service, and stewardship, we are driven by a heartfelt commitment to protect and serve others, inspired by our founders’ experiences – one as a United States Marine and the other as a Registered Nurse.
