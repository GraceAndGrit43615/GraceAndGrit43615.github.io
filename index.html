Our Mission

At Grace and Grit Defense Academy, our mission is to empower communities by equipping churches and daycare facilities with life-saving knowledge and skills through proven critical response techniques, guided by the values of ALERRT and C.R.A.S.E. Rooted in Christian values of compassion, service, and stewardship, we are driven by a commitment to protect and serve, inspired by our founders’ experiences as a U.S. Marine and a Registered Nurse.

We deliver exceptional training, emphasizing our ALERRT Train-the-Trainer certification, a nationally recognized program trusted for its effectiveness. We offer this training at no cost to ensure accessibility, aiming to foster safer environments where faith and care thrive.

Our Training: Avoid, Deny, Defend

Avoid

Create distance, escape danger. Learn situational awareness to safely avoid threats.

Deny

Block access, secure entry points. Master techniques to prevent threats from entering.

Defend

Use force as a last resort. Gain skills to protect when avoidance and denial fail.
