Mission Statement

At Grace & Grit Defense Academy, our mission is to empower communities by equipping churches and daycare facilities with life-saving knowledge and skills through proven critical response techniques, guided by the values of ALERRT and Citizen Response training. Rooted in Christian values of compassion, service, and stewardship, we protect and serve inspired by our founders’ experiences—one as a United States Marine and the other as a Registered Nurse.

Our Marine Corps background instills discipline, leadership, and duty, while our RN expertise brings care, crisis management, and human resilience. We leverage these strengths through our nationally recognized ALERRT Train-the-Trainer certification, ensuring the highest standards in critical incident preparedness.

We offer this training at no cost to churches and daycares, removing financial barriers and fostering safer environments where faith and care thrive. Serving others is our calling—building stronger, more resilient communities through education, empowerment, and unwavering commitment.
